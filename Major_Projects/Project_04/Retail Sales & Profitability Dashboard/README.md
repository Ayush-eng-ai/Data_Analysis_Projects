# 📊 Retail Sales & Profitability Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales & Profitability Dashboard** is an end-to-end business analytics project developed using **PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI** to analyze retail sales performance, profitability trends, and operational insights.

This project transforms raw Superstore sales data into meaningful business intelligence through:
- SQL-based data analysis
- Business KPI reporting
- Interactive Power BI dashboard creation
- Data visualization
- Presentation storytelling
- Dashboard demonstrations using images and GIFs

The project helps identify sales trends, profit drivers, discount impact, and customer behavior through interactive analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify high-performing and low-performing regions
- Evaluate category-wise profitability
- Study the impact of discounts on profit
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Build an interactive business dashboard
- Generate actionable business insights

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

## 🌍 Regional Performance
- West region generated the highest profit
- Central region showed weak profitability

---

## 💸 Discount Analysis
- Higher discounts negatively impacted profitability
- Discounts above 20% significantly reduced profit margins

---

## 📦 Category Insights
- Technology category performed strongest
- Furniture category showed lower profitability
- Tables sub-category generated losses

---

## 👥 Customer Segment Insights
- Consumer segment contributed the highest profit
- Corporate segment showed stable performance
